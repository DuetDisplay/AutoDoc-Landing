Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Auto-detected recording, on-device transcription, structured notes, full-text search, and a grounded Ask AI — all running on your machine.

01

The notes you'd write if you had time.

After every meeting, AutoDoc produces a structured summary organized into five categories.

✓
Decisions
What was decided, by whom, and the reasoning behind it.
✓
Action items
Tasks pulled from the conversation, with owner and deadline when mentioned.
✓
Information shared, discussion & status updates
Facts and figures, open questions and debates, and progress reports — each kept distinct.
✓
Editable after the fact
Refine notes and transcripts whenever you want. It's your data.
02

Capture everything, without a bot.

AutoDoc detects your calls and offers to start notes, then records screen, mic, and system audio together — locally, with no recorder joining the meeting.

✓
Automatic meeting detection
Spots Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, Slack, and Discord calls and offers to start notes — then auto-stops when the meeting ends.
✓
Multi-track recording
Screen video, your microphone, and system audio are captured as separate tracks for cleaner transcripts.
✓
Calendar auto-record
Connect Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook and set each meeting to record off, once, or for the whole series.
✓
Lives in your menu bar
Runs quietly in the background and shows your next meetings right from the tray. Works offline.
03

Find any moment.

Full-text search runs across everything you've captured and takes you straight to where it was said.

✓
Across transcripts and notes
Search every word in every transcript and every structured note at once.
✓
Jump to the exact moment
Click a result and AutoDoc opens the meeting, scrolls to the match, and highlights it.
✓
Instant, on-device
Searches your local library directly — nothing is sent anywhere to make it work.
Screenshot coming soon Ask AI — conversational Q&A
04

Ask your meetings.

A chat that answers questions grounded in your own meetings — not the open internet. "What did we commit to last week?" gets a real answer, drawn from your transcripts and notes.

✓
Conversational answers
Ask follow-up questions in plain English and get responses that stream back in a chat.
✓
Grounded across meetings
Answers are built from your transcripts and notes, pulling in the most relevant moments.
✓
Runs on a local model
Your questions and meeting content stay on your machine.
05

The full picture.

Notes on one side, the full transcript on the other. Audio and video playback synced to the text, speaker-aware transcripts, and edits whenever you need them.

✓
Synced playback
Skip, rewind, and scrub — the transcript follows along, and clicking text seeks the audio.
✓
Speaker identification
Color-coded speakers ("me" and "them" or Speaker 1, 2, 3…), with calendar attendees offered as rename suggestions.
✓
Inline editing
Fix a name, correct a quote, or add context — the transcript and notes are fully editable.
06

Private by architecture.

Privacy isn't a setting in AutoDoc — it's how the app is built. Processing happens on-device and your library is encrypted on disk.

✓
On-device transcription & notes
Audio and transcripts are never sent to the cloud for AI processing.
✓
AES-256-GCM at rest
Recordings, transcripts, and notes are encrypted on disk, with keys held in your macOS Keychain.
✓
Optional calendar, opt-in analytics
Calendar access is your choice, and diagnostics are off unless you turn them on.
