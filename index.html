Core features

Capture the meeting. Keep the meeting.

Summaries

The notes you'd write if you had time.

After every meeting, AutoDoc turns the conversation into structured notes — organized into five categories you can edit inline.

Decisions Action Items Information Shared Discussion Status Updates
Search

Find the exact moment.

Search across every transcript and note. Each result deep-links to the matching line, switches to the right tab, and highlights the phrase instantly.

Ask AI

Ask your meetings.

"What did we decide about the Q3 roadmap?" Ask in plain English and get answers grounded in what was actually said across one meeting or your full history. Separate from keyword search, powered by local Ollama.

What action items came out of yesterday's product sync?
From Product sync (Mar 14): Maya will tighten onboarding copy by Friday. Chris will add Windows build signing before the next beta.
Review

The full picture.

Video, audio, and transcript, synced and searchable. Speaker labels with me/them detection, calendar-attendee rename suggestions, synced playback, and inline editing.

Upcoming

Your calendar, ready to record.

Connect Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook to see upcoming meetings, auto-name recordings, and choose whether a meeting records once, for a whole recurring series, or only when you say so.
