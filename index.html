Meeting detection

It knows when you're in a meeting.

AutoDoc watches for calls in Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, Slack, and Discord. The moment one starts, a quiet prompt appears — "Start AI notes?" — one click and you're recording. No bot ever joins the call; AutoDoc captures audio right on your device, so nobody sees a recorder in the room. When the meeting ends, it stops on its own.

Why AutoDoc

Everything happens on your machine.

A complete meeting assistant — capture, transcription, notes, search, and answers — with nothing sent to the cloud to do it.

Structured notes

Every meeting organized into Decisions, Action Items, Information Shared, Discussion, and Status Updates — editable any time.

Search & Ask AI

Full-text search across every meeting, plus a chat that answers questions grounded in your own transcripts and notes.

Auto-detect meetings

Detects calls across all major platforms and offers to start notes — then stops automatically when the meeting wraps.

Multi-track capture

Records screen, your microphone, and system audio together — then identifies who said what with speaker labels.

On-device AI

Transcription and note-taking run locally with open models — no audio or transcript is sent anywhere for AI processing.

Encrypted on disk

Recordings, transcripts, and notes are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM, with keys held in your macOS Keychain.

Summaries

The notes you'd write if you had time.

Every meeting distilled into what actually matters — organized into Decisions, Action Items, Information Shared, Discussion, and Status Updates. Action items keep their owner and deadline. Everything is editable, so you can fix a name or add context after the fact.

Search

Find any moment.

Full-text search runs across every transcript and note you've captured. Match a phrase and jump straight to the exact moment it was said — the transcript scrolls to it and highlights it for you.

Ask AI

Ask your meetings.

"What did we decide about the Q3 roadmap?" Ask in plain English and AutoDoc answers in a chat, grounded in your own transcripts and notes across meetings. It runs against a local model, so your conversations stay on your machine while you get the answer.

Review

The full picture.

Video, audio, and a speaker-labeled transcript — synced and searchable. Scrub to any moment and the transcript follows. Rename speakers, fix a quote, edit anything after the fact. Your meetings become a living reference you can always go back to.

Upcoming

Your calendar, ready to record.

Connect Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook to see upcoming meetings, auto-name recordings from the event, and turn meeting attendees into speaker suggestions. Choose whether each meeting records off, once, or for the whole recurring series.

Privacy

Your meetings never leave your machine.

Transcription and AI notes run on-device — no audio or transcript is sent to the cloud to be processed. Everything is encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Calendar access is optional and analytics are opt-in.
