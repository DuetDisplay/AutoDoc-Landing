About

Meetings deserve better.

Every meeting tool we tried was built to feed data into someone else's AI platform. We wanted something different — structured notes, search, and Ask AI that run entirely on your machine, without making you dependent on big tech to do it.

From Duet to AutoDoc

We're the team behind Duet — a tool trusted by thousands. After years of building collaborative software, we kept running into the same problem: meetings generate the most important decisions in a company, but the tools for capturing them are either bad or exploitative.

AutoDoc is our take on what a meeting tool should be. Structured notes that surface what actually matters. Whisper and Ollama running locally — not in the cloud. Search when you need the exact phrase, Ask AI when you need the answer.

What we believe

Three principles.

Notes should
be useful.

A wall of transcript isn't a meeting note. AutoDoc organizes meetings into decisions, action items, information, discussion, and status updates — the things you'd actually write down if you had time.

Your data,
your machine.

Transcription, notes, and Ask AI run through Whisper and Ollama on your Mac. Calendar access is optional. Analytics are opt-in. Meeting content is encrypted at rest and never sent for cloud AI processing.

Independent
by design.

We're not building on top of someone else's AI platform. AutoDoc is AGPL-licensed and doesn't lock you into any ecosystem. The code will be yours to read, audit, and contribute to.
